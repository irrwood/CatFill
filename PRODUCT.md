# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who repeatedly complete job applications, visa forms, account profiles, and other structured web forms. They want to reuse trusted personal information quickly while staying in control of what is saved and sent to optional AI providers.

## Product Purpose

CatFill learns user-approved form details, organizes them locally, and fills similar forms on command. Success means less repetitive typing, accurate semantic matching across languages and form variations, and clear review before sensitive information is saved or sent.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, and friendly. The cat identity adds warmth, but the product remains a focused utility rather than a playful toy.

## Anti-references

Avoid card-within-card layouts, marketing-style hero composition, deep gray surfaces, harsh red states, oversized headings, verbose helper copy, decorative gradients, and controls that move or resize when their state changes.

## Design Principles

1. Put the user's current task first and keep the next action obvious.
2. Reveal advanced metadata and AI detail progressively instead of showing everything at once.
3. Keep saved personal information legible, editable, and visibly under user control.
4. Use familiar controls, stable dimensions, and restrained color to support repeated workflows.
5. Explain privacy and AI boundaries exactly where a user makes a related decision.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support Chinese and English, light and dark themes, keyboard focus, readable contrast, stable layouts at narrow side-panel widths, and reduced-motion preferences for nonessential transitions.
